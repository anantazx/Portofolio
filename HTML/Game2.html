Tales Untangled

About

Tales Untangled is a narrative-driven 2D platformer that brings Indonesian folklore to life. Blending platforming challenges with cultural exploration, the game offers an immersive and educational experience. Players unravel mystical tales, overcome obstacles, and discover the rich heritage woven into the game world.

Project info

  • Role : Gameplay Programmer
  • Team Size : 5
  • Engine : Unity
  • Duration : 5 Month

Introduction

Tales Untangled is a 2D narrative-driven platformer developed by a team of five under Infinite Learning. As the Game Programmer, I played a key role in bringing the game to life using Unity, ensuring smooth gameplay and an immersive player experience. My contributions focused on the technical aspects, optimizing mechanics, and creating a seamless, engaging journey through Indonesian folklore.

Explore & Platforming

In Tales Untangled, players navigate a diverse and immersive world filled with challenging platforming sequences and exploration-driven gameplay. Each level is uniquely designed featuring varied terrains, hidden secrets, and dynamic obstacles that require skill and strategy to overcome. The platforming mechanics are designed to evolve, introducing new movement abilities and environmental interactions that progressively enhance the gameplay experience.

Story-Driven Progression

In Tales Untangled, the narrative and gameplay are intricately woven together, making story progression a core element of the experience. Players step into the role of Aditya, a young bookworm who stumbles upon a mysterious book, only to find himself transported into a world where Indonesian folklore comes to life. To return home, Aditya must embark on a journey to recover scattered torn pages, each containing fragments of forgotten stories.

Interaction with Folklore Characters

In Tales Untangled, players engage with legendary figures from Indonesian folklore, each with their own unique stories, wisdom, and challenges. These interactions are not just for storytelling but are woven into the gameplay, offering quests, guidance, and obstacles that shape Adityaâ€™s journey.

Instead of simply narrating folklore, Tales Untangled lets players experience and influence these stories. Choices made during dialogues or actions taken in side quests impact character relationships and reveal alternate story outcomes.
