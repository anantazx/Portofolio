The lost Merchant

About

The Lost Merchant is a 3D survival adventure game developed as my final project in college, focusing on survival mechanics and strategic resource management. Players must navigate a hostile environment, manage limited resources.

Project info

  • Role : Gameplay Programmer
  • Team Size : 1
  • Engine : Unity
  • Duration : 1 Month

Introduction

The Lost Merchant is a survival adventure game that I developed as part of my final project. This project challenged me to design an immersive survival experience where players must endure one full in-game day, balancing resource management, combat, and strategic decision-making to stay alive.

Players take on the role of a lost merchant who finds themselves stranded in a hostile environment filled with unpredictable dangers. To survive, they must gather resources, and fend off relentless attacks from wild animals using either ranged combat (bow) or melee attacks (iron sword). The game emphasizes critical thinking and adaptability, requiring players to carefully manage their limited resources to sustain themselves until safety is secured.

Survival Mechanics

Players must carefully strategize resource management to survive one full in-game day in a hostile environment. Gathering essential supplies such as food, water, and materials is crucial to maintaining health and stamina. Every decision impacts survival—players must prioritize resources wisely, choosing between immediate consumption for sustenance or long-term planning for sustained survival.

In addition to managing resources, players must be mindful of environmental threats and adapt to changing conditions. Whether hunting for food, or finding shelter, each action plays a vital role in ensuring survival until the day ends. The game challenges players to think strategically, balancing risk and reward to maximize their chances of endurance.

Challenging Environment

The game presents a hostile and unpredictable environment where players must constantly adapt and strategize to survive. tigers roam the area, posing a constant threat that requires players to stay alert and manage their combat approach wisely—whether engaging in ranged attacks with a bow or opting for close combat with an iron sword.

Beyond combat, the environment itself introduces various survival challenges, such as limited resources, changing weather conditions, and hidden dangers. Players must explore cautiously, make strategic decisions, and utilize their surroundings effectively to overcome obstacles and endure the harsh survival experience.

Limited Resources

In The Lost Merchant, survival is not just about combat skills but also about managing scarce resources wisely. Players must carefully gather, conserve, and utilize essential supplies such as food, water, weapons to sustain themselves throughout one full in-game day. Every action matters—choosing when to consume resources for immediate survival or save them for potential future threats can determine success or failure.
